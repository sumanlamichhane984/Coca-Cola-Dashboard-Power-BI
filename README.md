# Coca-Cola-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing Coca-Cola sales data, including KPIs, trends, delivery trends and product performance.
Coca-Cola Sales & Logistics Dashboard�

This repository presents a comprehensive Power BI project analyzing Coca-Cola's sales performance, profitability, and logistics efficiency across regions and delivery partners. It includes interactive dashboards, datasets, and visual insights designed to support strategic business decisions.

* Dashboards Overview
1️Coca-Cola Sales Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of beverage brand performance.

Features:

1) Filters by brand and time period

2) Sales breakdown by brand (Coca-Cola, Diet Coke, Sprite, etc.)

3) Profitability metrics: Units Sold, Unit Price, Cost, Operating Margin, and Profit

4) AI-driven key influencers impacting profitability

5) Geographic sales distribution across the US

* Sales & Logistics Performance Tracker

Tracks delivery performance across regions and companies.

Key Metrics:

Total Deliveries: 3,744

New Delivery Companies: 4

Avg. Days to Deliver: 37.17

Visual Insights:

Regional trends: Map showing average delivery days (South region highest at 42.57)

Monthly delivery analysis: Bar chart by company and month

Delivery days distribution: Boxplot across companies

3️) Sales Report

Dives deeper into brand-wise and region-wise sales.

Visualizations:

Sales Tree Map: Distribution across Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Fanta, Sprite

Operating Profit Trends: Line chart over time

Profit by Brand: Bar chart comparison

Delivery Company Breakdown: Pie chart (FedEx, DHL, etc.)

Regional Sales Table: Sales figures and percentages for East, West, North, South

Detailed Region-Brand Table: Sales by brand within each region

Text Box: Commentary or insights
